YOURNEPHRON

Mission Objectives

YourNephron aims to promote renal health through a grassroots, community-based approach. We empower Californians by providing accessible education on kidney anatomy, function, and preventative wellness. By sharing credible information and creating engaging content, we raise awareness and encourage lifelong kidney health. Our mission is grounded in equity: we recognize that health literacy can vary across communities, and we’re committed to reaching everyone — from students learning about their bodies for the first time, to adults managing chronic conditions.


Who We Serve

Our resources are created for a wide audience — from college students taking their first anatomy course, to elderly individuals seeking guidance on maintaining kidney health as they age. We believe kidney education should be accessible, inclusive, and easy to understand for all people, regardless of their background or medical knowledge.
