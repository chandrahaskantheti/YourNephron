YOURNEPHRON

Mission Statement

YourNephron aims to promote renal health through a roots-up approach, empowering Californians with accessible education about kidney function, care & wellness. By fostering community engagement and sharing educational resources, we strive to enhance awareness and improve kidney health outcomes across the state.
